Let's Cook Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish featuring layers of pasta, savory meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. Baking enhances its rich, comforting flavors perfectly

Every layer contributes texture, taste, and aroma, creating a hearty, satisfying meal. Ideal for family gatherings or celebrations, lasagna offers indulgence, warmth, and deliciousness in every bite

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Sauce
  • Meat

Steps

  1. Prepare ingredients: Cook pasta sheets, make meat or veggie sauce, and mix béchamel or cheese filling
  2. Layer: In a baking dish, layer pasta, sauce, and cheese repeatedly until all ingredients are used
  3. Bake: Cook in a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F) for 30–40 minutes until golden, bubbly, and fully heated through
