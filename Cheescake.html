Let's Bake Cheesecake

Description

Cheesecake is a creamy dessert with a buttery crust, smooth cream cheese filling, and optional fruit or chocolate topping. Baking enhances its rich, velvety texture perfectly.

Every slice offers sweetness, creaminess, and elegance. Perfect for celebrations, gatherings, or a special treat, cheesecake delights with every bite, offering indulgence and satisfaction.

Ingredients

  • Cream cheese
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Graham cracker crumbs

Steps

  1. Prepare crust: Mix graham cracker crumbs with melted butter, press into a baking pan, and chill for 10 minutes
  2. Make filling: Beat cream cheese, sugar, and eggs until smooth, then pour over the crust evenly
  3. Bake: Cook in a preheated oven at 160°C (320°F) for 50–60 minutes until set. Cool, chill, and optionally top with fruit or chocolate
